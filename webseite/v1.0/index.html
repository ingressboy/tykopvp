TykoPVP your best PVP Minecraft Server!

TykoPvP is a small Minecraft server network on which there are various minigames. There is a nice Discord server and a nice website. There is also a forum where users ask questions to users. And much more!

What can you do on our server ?

You can fight against other players. Join with them in clan. Collect kills. Just spend your free time with us. Find and meet friends.
